Diabetes

Diabetes mellitus, or diabetes, is a serious chronic health condition which occurs when there is too much glucose in the blood. Insulin plays an important role in the body processing sugars from the blood stream. Thus, in people with diabetes insulin levels tend to be lower and blood sugars higher.
